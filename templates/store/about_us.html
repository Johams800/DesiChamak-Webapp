ABOUT DESI CHAMAK

Desi Chamak is a local retail company that sells South Asian accessories. The company sells its products online to customers worldwide.

Who Are We?


Sukhpreet Singh Nirvana Sharma Singh Gurpreet Singh

Greetings! We are three best friends that have started a flourishing local business in Virginia, USA known as Desi Chamak.

Growing up, we used to perform in the same Bhangra team. Bhangra is a traditional folk dance of Punjab, that connected and led us to create a business that best represents South Asian culture. Our friendship blossomed and soon led us to be here and provide the best products to customers like you!

In order to effectively advance the company to a higher and bigger platform, we have adopted numerous business growth strategies & techniques as a team throughout the years so you could have a user-friendly experience.

Mission

Our mission is to offer the best customer satisfaction to people around the NOVA area with the best South Asian Jewelry. We thrive and aim to double our base of new customers worldwide.

Goal

With our new Desi Chamak website, our goal is to overcome the business challenges that affect customer growth, customer satisfaction, and the visibility and modernism of the company. We have upgraded ourselves to bring Desi Chamak an online presence and automated our system to support customer needs and communication. Our effort is to provide the best shopping experience to not only domestic but also worldwide customers. In doing so, we are able to sell a higher quantity of products at an affordable price and deliver them to customers securely and free of shipping to those residing within the NOVA area.

OUR PRODUCTS

India is acknowledged as the largest and best center for cutting diamonds and gems in the world. Not only is the nation unmatched at cutting gems, but it also excels at producing exceptional jewelry, whether it is fashioned by hand or with machinery.

Desi Chamak provides Indian handmade jewelry to those interested. Our products are made from premium materials and are as elegant as they can be to promise a dashing look to those who wear them.

Besides that, we also stock other pieces of fashion other than gold. We showcase different bundles of products across our website. Please feel free to check them out.

If you have any concerns, feedback, or questions, please reach out to us on the Contact Us page.

We thank you for choosing Desi Chamak!
