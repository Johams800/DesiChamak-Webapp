ABOUT DESI CHAMAK

Desi Chamak is a local retail company that sells South Asian accessories. The company sells its products online to customers worldwide.

Who Are We?

Sukhpreet Singh Nirvana Sharma Singh Gurpreet Singh

Greetings! We are three best friends that have found a flourishing local business in Virginia, USA known as Desi Chamak.

During our college days, we used to perform in the team name Bhangra Dance group. Bhangra is a traditional folk dance of Punjab in India. Our friendship blossomed and soon led us to create a business that best represents South Asian culture.

In order to effectively advance the company to a higher and bigger platform, we have adopted numerous business growth strategies & techniques as a team throughout the years.

OUR PRODUCTS

India is acknowledged as the largest and best center for cutting diamonds and gems in the world. Not only is the nation unmatched at cutting gems, but it also excels at producing exceptional jewelry, whether it is fashioned by hand or with machinery.

Desi Chamak provides Indian handmade jewelry to those interested. Our products are made from premium materials and as elegant as it can be to promise a dashing look to those who wear it.

The gold of our nations looks heavy in the gold color and all our products utilize 24 carat gold jewelry, which has a fine yellow hue. They also blend alloys to create fancy and durable jewelry.

Besides that, we also stock other pieces of fashion other than gold. We showcase different bundles of products across our website. Please feel free to check them out.
